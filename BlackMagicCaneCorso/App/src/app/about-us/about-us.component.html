About Black Magic Cane Corsos I am a small hobby breeder located in North East Ohio close to PA. We are within 3 Hours of MI and NY. I am that is also a preservation breeder meaning that I abide by our breed standard and strive to preserve the integrity of the breed. I am a member of multiple Cane Corso clubs like ICCF, CCAA and also am an AKC CGC Evaluator. Voodoo was my first Corso and I absolutely fell in love with the breed and I became so involved, after much study, I decided to become a breeder. I have only had a couple litters and do not breed frequently so I can focus on titling my dogs. I owner handle all my dogs in shows and also do all my own training. I like to keep personal relationships with everyone that gets a puppy from me. I am here for my puppy owners always and offer health guarantees and life time support.
