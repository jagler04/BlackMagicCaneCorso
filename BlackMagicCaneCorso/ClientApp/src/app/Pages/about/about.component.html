About:
I have been a breeder owner handler for 52 years. I acquired my first Black Russian Terrier in 2007. I am the first AKC Breed ambassador for the Black Russian Terrier. Previously I owned Giant Schnauzers, but wanted something with less drive but similar looks. The BRT, as they are affectionately called, was my answer. I appreciate the breed for their adaptability, intelligence, protection abilities and intuitiveness. They are a wonderful family dog; bonding closely with their people and are aloof of strangers. Don't expect this breed to be welcoming of strangers on your property. We have a number of UKC and AKC CHs on site and all adult have temperament titles. Brt-ology believes in breed type temperament first, followed closely by health. All puppies and dogs are raised indoors, socialized properly and enjoy the rural property. BRT-OLOGY is located in beautiful Ohio countryside near Alliance, OHIO.
